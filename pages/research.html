About My Research 🚗

Nimbus Lab

The NIMBUS (Nebraska Intelligent MoBile Unmanned Systems) Lab is an exciting place where the latest research and technology in software and systems engineering, robotics, and sensor networks converges to develop more capable and dependable UAVs.

TransSafeAI Group

Our research interests are reachability analysis and robustness certification of deep neural networks (DNN), formal verification of autonomous cyber-physical systems(CPS) with learning-enabled components, safe and robust training for DNN, real-time verification and safe motion planning for distributed CPS. We are also interested in robust control, stability analysis of nonlinear control systems, and networked control systems.
