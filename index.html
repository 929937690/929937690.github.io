I am a second year Ph.D. student in the Computer Science and Engineering Department at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln. I work as a Graduate Research Assistant in the School of Computing. I am a part of TransSafeAI research group led by my advisor Dr. Dung Tran at the Nimbus Lab in the Deptartment of Computer Science. I am also co-advised by Dr. Marilyn Wolf. My research interest include verifying deep neural networks and learning‑enabled cyber‑physical systems, and formal techniques for modeling, recognizing, localizing, and mitigating cyberattacks in autonomous systems built based on robotic operating systems (ROS).

Before joining UNL, I earned a B.S. degree in Computer Science from University of Nebraska-Kearney.
